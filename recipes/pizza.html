Easy Homemade Pizza



Description

When making homemade pizza, one of the most difficult things to do is waiting for the dough to rise.

LUCKILY, with THIS recipe, YOU DO NOT HAVE TO WAIT LONG!

The longest you need to wait is ONLY 5 Minutes!

A delicious, soft and chewy pizza awaits!


Ingredients

All you need is five ingredients! Check it out:

  • 1 Cup Warm Water
  • .25oz Yeast
  • 1tsp Sugar
  • 2.5 cups Bread Flour
  • 2tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1tsp Salt
  • Desired Toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F (230 C) and lightly grease a pizza pan
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix until creamy and let stand for 10 mins.
  3. Add flour, oil and salt to yeast mixture. beat until smooth.
  4. Let mixture rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Roll out dough into 12-inch circle.
  6. Spread with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
