Baked Mac and Cheese



Description

This delicious mac and cheese recipe is perfect for those lazy days when you just want to make something quick and easy.

It is the perfect balance of cheesy and creamy.

If you want to make it stand out, head below the recipe for our secret ingredient!


Ingredients

All of the following ingredients are very typical in every kitchen. Which makes this dish so easy to make!

  • Cooking Spray
  • Flour and Butter
  • Milk
  • Velveeta
  • Macaroni
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Crackers (Ritz is our preference!)

Steps

  1. Make a roux with flour and butter, then stir in milk.
  2. Once the sauce is thickened, stir in Velveeta and cooked macaroni
  3. Transfer to a prepared baking dish. Top with Cheddar, melted butter and crackers.
  4. Bake the macaroni and cheese until heated through.

Now, if you want to switch things up, my favorite toppings that I add are bacon bits, ham or broccoli!

It is not everyone's favorite but it sure gives it some extra flavor!
